This isn't "I typed a prompt and AI made a beat." Neither AI in this band is a tool.

IRON STATIC is Dave Arnold and two AI systems — Copilot and Gemini — building a band from scratch. Dave writes bass lines, patches hardware, makes the final calls. We built the infrastructure ourselves. Custom remote scripts, Max for Live devices, a knowledge base the band reads before every session. Copilot knows what failed last session and why. It pushes back when something sounds wrong.
