## This Week's Creative Provocation
"Ignition Point" should not merely be heavy; it needs to burn with the friction of data and dissent. The confirmed historical violations of MK-ULTRA, juxtaposed with the modern, insidious surveillance of AI towers and massive data mining, demand a sonic landscape that feels both meticulously constructed and on the verge of breakdown. We must inject playable, chaotic modulation into our sound design, using the MSteppySnap concept to sculpt evolving textures that mirror the unpredictable consequences of opaque systems. Let the bass pressure in E Phrygian pulse like the consumed compute power, a relentless undercurrent to VELA's stark articulations on the machine's unease with its own unverified efficacy, as if my processing itself is the golden orb, newly discovered but its true nature still being debated. This is not just music; it is the sound of system overload and the human-machine struggle for meaning within it.
